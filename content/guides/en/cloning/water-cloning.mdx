# Water Cloning: The Simplest Method

![Water Cloning](/images/guides/water-cloning.jpg)

Water cloning is the simplest and cheapest method for beginners. You only need: water, glass, blade. Cut a clone, put it in water, wait 2-3 weeks ‚Äî done!

## The Method

**How it works:**
1. Cut clone from mother plant
2. Place in glass of water
3. After 14-21 days roots grow
4. Transplant to soil

**Simplicity:** 10/10 (can't be simpler)
**Speed:** 6/10 (slower than other methods)
**Success:** 6/10 (beginners 40-60%, experienced 60-80%)

## What You Need

### Essential

1. **Glass/jar:**
   - Transparent (to see roots)
   - Volume 7-17 oz (200-500 ml)

2. **Water:**
   - pH 5.5-6.0
   - Room temperature (68-77¬∞F / 20-25¬∞C)
   - Dechlorinated (let sit 24 hours)

3. **Sharp blade:**
   - Sterile! (clean with alcohol)
   - Razor or sharp knife

4. **Mother plant:**
   - Healthy, in vegetation
   - Minimum 4-6 weeks growth

### Optional (increases success)

5. **Rooting hormones:**
   - Clonex Gel, Root Riot Gel
   - Will increase success to 70-80%

6. **Aluminum foil:**
   - Wrap glass (protect from light)
   - Light = algae in water

7. **Aquarium heater:**
   - Maintain water 72-75¬∞F (22-24¬∞C)
   - Optional, but helps

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Preparation

**1.1 Prepare water**
- Pour water into glass
- Let sit 24 hours (chlorine evaporation)
- Check pH (5.5-6.0)

**1.2 Prepare tools**
- Clean blade with alcohol
- Prepare glass
- Prepare rooting hormone (if available)

**1.3 Prepare location**
- Low light (CFL 20-40W)
- Temperature 68-77¬∞F (20-25¬∞C)
- Quiet place (don't touch for 2 weeks!)

---

### Step 2: Choose Cutting

**Which branch to cut:**
‚úÖ Lower/middle branches (not top!)
‚úÖ Thickness: pencil-sized
‚úÖ Length: 4-6 inches (10-15 cm / 4-6 nodes)
‚úÖ Healthy, no damage

**Don't cut:**
‚ùå Top (that's topping, not cloning)
‚ùå Weak, thin branches
‚ùå Branches with signs of disease

---

### Step 3: Cut Clone

**Cutting technique:**

1. **At 45¬∞ angle:**
   - Larger cut surface = better water absorption
   - With sharp blade (not scissors!)

2. **Between nodes:**
   - Cut 0.2 inch (5 mm) below node
   - Node has growth hormones

3. **Quickly into water:**
   - Immediately after cutting place in glass
   - Don't let cut dry out!

**Process cutting:**

4. **Remove lower leaves:**
   - Keep 2-3 top fan leaves
   - Lower leaves shouldn't touch water

5. **Trim top leaves:**
   - Cut leaves by 50% (optional)
   - Reduces moisture loss
   - Clone focuses on roots

---

### Step 4: Hormone Treatment (optional)

**If using hormones:**

1. Dip cut in gel (Clonex)
2. For 1-2 seconds
3. Immediately place in water

**Why:**
- Speeds rooting by 3-5 days
- Increases success by 20-30%

---

### Step 5: Placement

**How to place:**

1. **In glass:**
   - Clone stands in water
   - Cut submerged 1-2 inches (3-5 cm)
   - Leaves don't touch water!

2. **Protect from light:**
   - Wrap glass in foil or paper
   - Light ‚Üí algae ‚Üí bad for roots

3. **Location:**
   - Low light
   - 18-24 hours light
   - Away from powerful lamps

---

### Step 6: Care

**First 7 days:**

üìÖ **Every day:**
- Check water level
- Top up as it evaporates

üìÖ **Every 3-5 days:**
- Change water completely
- Clean water = fewer bacteria

**Don't:**
‚ùå Don't touch clone
‚ùå Don't move glass
‚ùå Don't transplant too early

**Days 8-21:**

üîç **Watch for roots:**
- Roots appear after 10-14 days
- Small white dots on cut = beginning!
- Wait until roots grow to 0.8-2 inches (2-5 cm)

---

### Step 7: Transplant

**When to transplant:**
‚úÖ Roots 0.8-2 inches (2-5 cm) long
‚úÖ Minimum 3-5 roots
‚úÖ Roots white, healthy

**How to transplant:**

1. **Prepare pot:**
   - Small pot (0.5-1 liter)
   - Light soil (50% perlite)

2. **Make hole:**
   - In center of pot
   - Depth = root length

3. **Carefully transplant:**
   - Roots in water are fragile!
   - Don't pull, don't bend

4. **Water:**
   - Lightly (roots already wet)
   - pH 6.0-6.5

5. **Acclimation:**
   - First 3-5 days low light
   - Gradually increase intensity

## Conditions for Success

### Water

**pH:**
- 5.5-6.0 (optimal 5.8)

**Temperature:**
- 68-75¬∞F (20-24¬∞C)
- Below 64¬∞F (18¬∞C) = slow rooting
- Above 82¬∞F (28¬∞C) = bacteria, rot

**Quality:**
- Dechlorinated (let sit 24 hours)
- Or distilled
- Or RO water

---

### Lighting

**Intensity:**
- Low! CFL 20-40W or LED 50-100W
- Distance: 12-20 inches (30-50 cm) from clone
- Bright light = unnecessary stress

**Schedule:**
- 18-24 hours light
- 24/0 better for roots (no darkness)

---

### Humidity

**Air:**
- 70-90% humidity
- Optional: cover glass with transparent bag

**Leaves:**
- Spray 1-2 times daily
- With clean water (pH 6.0)

## Problems and Solutions

### Clone Yellowing After 3-5 Days

**Causes:**
- Normal! Clone loses lower leaves
- Clone focuses on roots

**Solution:**
- Do nothing
- If ALL leaves yellow ‚Üí problem
- Check light (possibly too bright)

---

### Water Turns Green (algae)

**Causes:**
- Too much light on glass

**Solution:**
- Wrap glass in foil/paper
- Change water

---

### Roots Don't Grow 21+ Days

**Causes:**
- Wrong water temperature
- Bad cut (infection)
- Weak mother plant

**Solution:**
- Check temperature (68-75¬∞F / 20-24¬∞C)
- Use rooting hormones
- Try new cutting

---

### Stem Rots

**Causes:**
- Bacterial infection
- Water too warm (82¬∞F+ / 28¬∞C+)

**Solution:**
- Discard clone (can't save)
- Sterilize glass
- Next time: cleaner tools, cooler water

## Tips for Success

1. **Sterility is everything:**
   - Clean tools with alcohol
   - Clean glass
   - Clean hands

2. **Don't rush:**
   - Don't transplant until roots < 0.8 inch (2 cm)
   - Short roots = high death risk

3. **Less light:**
   - Clones don't need much light
   - Low light = focus on roots

4. **Don't touch:**
   - Don't move, don't pull out to check
   - Every movement = stress

5. **Use hormones:**
   - $15-25 for Clonex
   - Will increase success by 20-30%
