# Rooting Hormones: Complete Guide to Selection and Use

![Cloning Hormones](/images/guides/cloning-hormones.jpg)

Rooting hormones are substances that stimulate root growth in cuttings. One cut = thousands of cells ready to become roots. Hormones "activate" these cells. Result: roots 3-5 days faster + success up 20-30%. Clonex Gel is the gold standard, but there are cheaper alternatives!

## What Are Rooting Hormones?

**Definition:**
- Synthetic or natural substances
- Stimulate cell division in cut zone
- Speed up root formation

**Main ingredient: IBA (Indole-3-butyric acid)**
- This is an auxin (plant hormone)
- Makes cells transform into roots
- Naturally present in plants (but in small doses)

**How it works:**
1. Apply hormone to cut
2. IBA penetrates tissues
3. Cells receive signal "grow roots!"
4. Roots appear 3-5 days faster

## Why Hormones Needed?

**Without hormones:**
- Clone roots in 10-14 days (water/soil)
- Success: 60-70%

**With hormones:**
- Clone roots in 7-10 days
- Success: 80-90%

**Difference:**
- +20-30% to success
- -3-5 days time
- Roots stronger, denser

**Are hormones required?**
- No! Plants can root without them
- But: with hormones easier, faster, more reliable
- Especially for beginners

## Types of Rooting Hormones

### 1. Gel (Clonex Gel, Root Riot Gel)

**What it is:**
- Thick gel with IBA
- Coats cut, doesn't wash off in water

**IBA concentration:**
- 3000 ppm (0.3%)

**Pros:**
- Sticks to cut (doesn't wash off in water/soil)
- Easy to use (dip and done)
- Contains nutrients, vitamins

**Cons:**
- More expensive than powder ($15-25 vs $5-10)
- Shelf life 1-2 years (powder lasts longer)

**For:**
- All levels
- Especially beginners (easiest to use)

---

### 2. Powder (Hormex, Root Riot Powder)

**What it is:**
- Dry powder with IBA

**IBA concentration:**
- Various: 1000 ppm, 3000 ppm, 8000 ppm
- For cloning: 3000 ppm (like gel)

**Pros:**
- Cheaper than gel ($5-10)
- Long shelf life (3-5 years)
- Can choose concentration

**Cons:**
- Harder to use (need to moisten cut)
- Can fall off

**For:**
- Intermediate/advanced level
- Those who clone regularly (economy)

---

### 3. Liquid (Clonex Solution, Olivia's Cloning Solution)

**What it is:**
- Liquid IBA solution
- Added to water (aerocloner, hydro)

**IBA concentration:**
- 100-300 ppm (diluted 1:10 or 1:20)

**Pros:**
- Perfect for aerocloner
- Long shelf life
- Economical (diluted)

**Cons:**
- Not suitable for soil (washes off)
- Need to dilute (bit harder)

**For:**
- Aerocloner, hydroponics
- Advanced level

## How to Use Hormones

### Gel (Clonex, Olivia's)

**Step by step:**

1. **Prepare cutting:**
   - Cut at 45¬∞ angle (sharp blade)
   - Immediately place in glass of water

2. **Apply gel:**
   - Remove cutting from water
   - Dip cut in gel 0.4-0.8 inch (1-2 cm)
   - Hold 2-3 seconds

3. **Place in medium:**
   - Right after gel ‚Üí into soil/coco/Rapid Rooter
   - Don't let cut dry out!

4. **Don't touch:**
   - Gel sticks to cut, works 7-14 days

**Important:**
- DON'T dip cutting directly into jar!
- Squeeze some into lid (to keep jar clean)
- Use fresh portion each time

---

### Powder (Hormex, TakeRoot)

**Step by step:**

1. **Prepare cutting:**
   - Cut at 45¬∞ angle
   - Place in water

2. **Moisten cut:**
   - Remove from water
   - Shake excess water (cut should be moist, not wet!)

3. **Apply powder:**
   - Dip cut in powder (0.4-0.8 in / 1-2 cm)
   - Shake off excess (light tap)
   - Should leave thin layer

4. **Place in medium:**
   - Right into soil/coco
   - Don't let dry!

**Important:**
- If cut too wet ‚Üí powder won't stick
- If too dry ‚Üí powder falls off
- Perfect: moist but not dripping

---

### Liquid (Clonex Solution)

**For aerocloner:**

1. **Dilute:**
   - 0.17-0.33 oz per quart (5-10 ml per L) of water
   - pH 5.5-6.0

2. **Fill reservoir:**
   - Completely fill reservoir with solution

3. **Place cuttings:**
   - As usual in aerocloner
   - Misters spray solution with hormone

4. **Change solution:**
   - Every 3-5 days fresh solution

**For hydroponics:**
- Same (add to nutrient solution)

## Storage and Shelf Life

**Gel (Clonex):**
- Shelf life: 1-2 years
- Store: room temperature, dark place
- Close tightly (dries out)

**Powder (Hormex):**
- Shelf life: 3-5 years
- Store: dry cool place
- Lasts very long!

**Liquid (Clonex Solution):**
- Shelf life: 2-3 years
- Store: room temperature
- Diluted solution: 1-2 weeks (in fridge)

**Signs hormone went bad:**
- Gel: changed color (became dark)
- Powder: lumps, wet
- Liquid: cloudy, sediment

## Safety

**For humans:**
- IBA is mildly toxic
- But: wear gloves (irritates skin)
- Don't eat, don't inhale powder

**For plants:**
- Safe at correct dosage
- Overdose: roots may not grow

**Environment:**
- IBA biodegrades
- Not toxic to soil/water
