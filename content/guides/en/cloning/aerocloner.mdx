# Aerocloner: Professional Cloning

![Aerocloner](/images/guides/aerocloner.jpg)

Aerocloner is the fastest and most effective cloning method. Cuttings hang in air, roots are sprayed with pressurized water. Result: 90-100% success in 5-10 days. Perfect for commercial growers and those who clone regularly.

## What is an Aerocloner?

**How it works:**
- Cuttings secured in neoprene inserts (collars)
- Stems hang in air inside container
- Root zone sprayed with water from misters 24/7
- Roots grow in air = maximum aeration + growth

**Why it works better:**
- Roots get 100% oxygen (in soil 10-20%)
- Constant moisture (doesn't dry out)
- No overwatering risk
- Perfect water temperature

## How Aerocloner Works

### Main Components

**1. Container (reservoir):**
- Holds water (5-20 L)
- Light-proof (black/dark)
- With lid

**2. Lid:**
- With holes for neoprene inserts
- Distance between holes: 3-4 inches (7-10 cm)

**3. Neoprene inserts (collars):**
- Hold cutting vertically
- Size: 2 inches (5 cm) diameter (standard)
- Soft, don't pinch stem

**4. Pump:**
- Power: 100-200 GPH (400-800 L/h, depends on size)
- Silent
- Submersible (inside reservoir)

**5. Misters (sprayers):**
- Create fine mist
- Spray root zone 360Â°
- 1 mister per 4-8 cuttings

**6. Tubing:**
- Connects pump to misters
- Diameter: 0.4-0.6 inch (1-1.5 cm)

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Assembly (for DIY)

**1.1 Prepare container:**
- Container 5-10 gal (20-40 L, for 10-20 clones)

- Diameter: 2 inches (5 cm, for neoprene inserts)

- Misters on tubes

## Problems and Solutions

### Water Turns Green (algae)

**Causes:**
- Light enters reservoir
- Water too warm

**Solution:**
- Use opaque container (black)
- Wrap in foil if transparent
- Cool water to 68-72Â°F (20-22Â°C)
- Add H2O2 (0.1 oz/qt / 3 ml/L)

---

### Roots Brown/Slimy

**Cause:**
- Root rot (Pythium)
- Water too warm
- Dirty system

**Solution:**
- Discard infected clones
- Completely clean system (3% peroxide)
- Change water
- Add H2O2
- Cool water to 64-68Â°F (18-20Â°C)

---

### Misters Clogged

**Cause:**
- Mineral deposits
- Debris in water

**Solution:**
- Remove misters
- Rinse under tap
- Soak in vinegar (if minerals)
- Use filter on pump

---

### Roots Don't Grow 10+ Days
