## Stealth Growing (Complete Concealment)

### Location

**Best places for tent**:
- Walk-in closet / storage room
- Attic / basement
- Bedroom corner (disguised as wardrobe)
- Garage (if heated)

**Bad places**:
- Room with windows to street
- Places with frequent guests
- Near shared walls (neighbors hear)

### Camouflage

**Ideas for hiding tent**:
- Converted wardrobe / closet
- "Server room" with computers (explains noise and light)
- Hobby room (for "tomato seedlings")
- Storage room with lock

**What to say when questioned**:
- "Growing tomatoes/peppers"
- "Mining servers" (explains noise)
- "Meat drying chamber" (explains smell)

## Risks When Renting

**Additional risks**:
- Landlord may enter without notice
- Neighbors complain more often
- Meter inspections

**Precautions**:
- Grow only in long-term rental (year+)
- Don't give landlord keys to room with tent
- Ensure soundproofing and smell control
- Consider insurance for lost deposit
